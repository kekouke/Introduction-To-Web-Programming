MEET THE TEAM

Natalia

Natalia is the co-founder, visual idealist and driving force of Killmonday Games. She is a professional illustrator and animator with a huge passion for characters and writing. She also has a background in acting and is the voice behind the charming little lady Misfortune from ”Little Misfortune”. Natalia is also founder of Killmonday Shop. She loves cats, horror and all things creative.

Isak

Isak is the co-founder, musical magician and mad idea machine of Killmonday Games. He is a professional composer and programmer and is also writing for the games they make. Besides game design, he’s also pursuing a career as a professional space ninja.

Essi

Essi is a professional 2D and 3D artist who paints and builds models at the speed of light. She is very multifaceted in art, and she is especially excellent at designing cute and creepy characters for the games. Essi also happens to have a profound affection for dinosaurs.

Harald

Harald is a professional programmer with a deep passion for code, code and also code. He specializes in problem-solving and is a professional bug-killer (of digital ones, not real). Sometimes when he works, the keyboard is lit on fire from all the intense coding.

Anton

Anton is a professional programmer whose power lies in visual effects and environmental animation. He works silently, but forcefully. Always striving for excellence and he is a bug hunter of the highest class. He is also a proud mother of a sweet little kitten family.

Elsa

Elsa is a professional 2D artist and ”all-arounder”, who is often found working on at least 10 things at a time (well, almost, at least). Most of the content you see on Killmonday Games’ various social media accounts has been created by her. She loves to paint and helps create art for the games and merch.
